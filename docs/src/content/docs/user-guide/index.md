## Who This Guide Is For

This section is for operators and traders using the terminal app day-to-day.

You will find practical workflows for:

- navigating the workspace quickly
- opening and managing orders safely
- monitoring positions and alerts
- using assistant, automation, and messaging tools
- managing wallet, auth, and settings

## Operational Baseline

Before placing orders, make sure:

- wallet is connected and funded
- selected market and outcome are correct
- order type and post-only options are intentional
- active overlays are closed when using global shortcuts

## Fast Entry Points

- press `o` to open buy form for current outcome
- press `s` to open sell form for current outcome
- press `h` to inspect open orders and trade history
- press `p` for portfolio panel
- press `z` for alerts panel
- press `e` for settings
